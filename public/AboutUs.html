Arioso Cafe is a small roaster based in Vancouver, Canada. The Chief Roaster, Don Parkhurst has been small batch roasting for family, friends as well as for his own personal enjoyment, for almost 20 years.

Don’s other job as an airline pilot for the last 30 years, has had him travelling around the world and enjoying espresso at some of the finest coffee houses on the planet. Aside from enjoying the hand-crafted espresso drinks at some excellent cafes, it also inspires and drives him to create the finest roast possible whether it is for our own unique espresso blend (Bossa Nova Espresso) or any of our single origin coffees.

At Arioso we only buy select small lots of fair trade/fair trade organic beans, which has allowed us to keep the highest possible quality in our roasts and to ensure a fair return for the great product grown by these farmers.
